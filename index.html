About Me


HI! My name is Cristian and I enjoy working with all types of tech. My interset started when I built my first gaming computer and then converted into a hackintosh. I have been working with all types of tech since then. One of my passion is to write code to help with productivity or just build a website for a friend.

Fast forward to today, and I've the privilega of implmenting software using cloud technologies tools, and working with opensource apps to help my current employer with productivity for employees.

I also work on desinning networks for business who need to virtualization and cloud services for their business needs.
